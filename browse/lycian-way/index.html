Lycian way

The Lycian Way is a long-distance footpath in Turkey along the coast of ancient Lycia. It is approximately 510 km long collection of ancient paths, mule and caravan trails, forest and country roads with many Lycian and Roman sites on its route. You can discover beautiful landscapes and a fascinating history of Mediterranean Turkey.
