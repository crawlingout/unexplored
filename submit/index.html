submit your images

If you want to submit your pictures for publication, you can send them to unexploredparadises@gmail.com.
You can also send a link to images at Flickr if they are under a permissive license.
Even without images, a tip for others about some interesting place would be greatly appreciated.

You can include several photos from the same trip. Preferably geotagged - we need to know the location of the unexplored paradise you discovered. If your photographs are not geotagged, please tell us where they are from.

If you want a link to your website (for example if you wish to promote your travel blog), include your URL.

Please make sure that you have all necessary rights and permissions to submit these photographs and have them posted. We do not want to violate any intellectual property rights of any third party.

Thank you.
